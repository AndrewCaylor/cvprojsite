Abstract

According to the American Automobile Association, pothole damage cost US drivers over $15 billion in damages over the last five years. Also, two-thirds of Americans are concerned with potholes on their roads [2]. As self-driving cars become more prevalent, it might become necessary for them to be programmed to avoid potholes in order to prevent damage to the vehicle [1]. The goal of this project is to find the locations of potholes in an image, and then find the relative location to the camera.
